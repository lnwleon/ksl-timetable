Passing Strategy

Oral Exams — 20 Jul

Speak answers aloud daily. Mock orals with classmates. Procedure questions are key for all 9 units.

Past Papers (2015+)

Work every paper. Time yourself strictly. Master recurring topics first — patterns repeat.

★ LWD — Top Priority

Nightly drafting block is non-negotiable. Draft pleadings, conveyances, wills and commercial agreements every night.

🟢 Saturday = Full Rest

Your brain consolidates memory on rest days. Saturday is protected. No studying.

2 Hours Per Unit Min.

Never less than 2hrs per unit per sitting. Depth beats coverage every time.

Nov Mains Rotation

After orals, rotate all 9 units weekly. Revisit your weakest units twice.
